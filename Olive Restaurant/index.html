About OLIVE

OLIVE celebrates seasonal, bright Mediterranean flavors — handmade sauces, fresh breads, locally-sourced produce and sustainable seafood. Our menu reflects coastal inspiration and classic family recipes.

Meet Chef Maria Rossi

Chef Maria Rossi
Head Chef & Co-owner

Inspired by coastal traditions, Chef Maria focuses on clean flavors and seasonal produce.

Our Philosophy

We believe in honest food — simple techniques that highlight the ingredient. Expect wood-fired notes, bright dressings and dishes that feel both familiar and exciting.
